Dental

Dental Coverage

Depending on your and your family’s needs, you can pick between two dental plans (Core and Buy Up Plans) through MetLife.

Both plans permit you to use both in-network and out-of-network providers. Network providers offer larger discounts and can file your claims for you. If you prefer to see an out-of-network PPO provider, keep in mind, because they are not contracted to accept the negotiated discounted rate, they may charge you for any amount billed in excess of the negotiated discounted rate.

To find an in-network provider:

  • Visit MetLife.com
  • Click on “Find a Dentist”
  • Select the network “PDP Plus”
  • Enter your city, state or zip code
  • You can then search by provider name, practice name, or location.
ID cards are not needed to receive services, but one will be mailed to your home.
You can also access an image of your ID card by logging on to www.metlife.com/mybenefits or through the MetLife Mobile App.
