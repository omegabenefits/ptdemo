Depending on your and your family\u2019s needs, you can pick between two dental plans (Core <\/strong>and Buy Up Plans<\/strong>) through MetLife.<\/p>

Both plans permit you to use both in-network and out-of-network providers. Network providers offer larger discounts and can file your claims for you. If you prefer to see an out-of-network PPO provider, keep in mind, because they are not contracted to accept the negotiated discounted rate, they may charge you for any amount billed in excess of the negotiated discounted rate.\u00a0<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
