The PT Solutions 401(k) Retirement Savings Plan offers a convenient way for you to save for the future through pretax payroll deductions. You are eligible to participate in the plan as of January 1 or July 1 following 60-days of employment.<\/p>

Your Contributions:<\/strong> Contributions from your pay are made on a pre-tax basis — up to the IRS annual limit.<\/p>

If you are 50 years of age or older (or if you will reach age 50 by the end
of the year), you may make a catch-up contribution in addition to the normal IRS annual limit.<\/p>

You may also make post-tax Roth 401(k) contributions, provided that the total of your pre-tax and post-tax contributions do not exceed IRS maximums.<\/p>
