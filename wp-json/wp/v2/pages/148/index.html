    PT Solutions offers three health plan options through Anthem \/ Blue Cross Blue Shield of Georgia (Anthem\/BCBS). All three plans include prescription medication coverage.<\/strong><\/p>

    You have an option to enroll in the Premium Plan<\/strong>, Plus Plan<\/strong> or in the Value Plan<\/strong> which is a High Deductible Health Plan and includes a Health Savings Account (HSA).<\/p>

    All three plans offer out of network benefits giving you the flexibility to use an in-network or out-of-network physician. To maximize your benefits and reduce your out of pocket expenses, you should try to select an in network provider. For care received out of network, the provider may bill you for amounts exceeding the negotiated discounted rate.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
